What is Google Ads & Its Benefits?

Google Ads is an advertising platform where businesses promote their products or services on Google’s search engine results page (SERP) and other Google properties. It’s the most effective way to reach potential customers who are already searching for what you offer.

Benefits for Businesses:

  • Increased visibility on Google search results.
  • Highly targeted advertising to reach the ideal customer.
  • Measurable results and clear ROI tracking.
  • Scalable campaigns as per business needs and budget.
